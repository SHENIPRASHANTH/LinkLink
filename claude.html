Who Can Donate?

Age Criteria

While there is no strict age limit for organ donation, healthy individuals in the age range of 18 to 65 are typically eligible for organ donation.

Health Conditions

Healthy individuals with no history of severe chronic illnesses, such as cancer or heart disease, can be considered for donation.

Medical Screening

Potential donors undergo a thorough medical evaluation, including blood tests and organ function tests, to ensure compatibility and viability.

Living Donors

Living individuals who are willing to donate a kidney, partial liver, or other tissues must be in good health and undergo a comprehensive medical assessment.

Brain Death Criteria

Individuals declared brain-dead due to severe trauma or illness may be eligible for organ donation, even if their heart is still beating.

Donor Family Consent

In the case of deceased donors, family consent is required to proceed with the organ donation process.
